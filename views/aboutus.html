ABOUT SYSTEM

This is web application for fake invoice detection. Our team has developed a web application that is designed to automate the process of detecting invoices from Images. The purpose of this software is to help businesses and individuals streamline their invoicing processes by reducing the time and effort required to manually identify and extract relevant information from invoices. Our application uses state-of-the-art machine learning algorithms to recognize and extract important information such as the date, vendor name, and total amount due from the uploaded invoices. We believe that our project will make a valuable contribution to the field of Finance automation and we are excited to share it with the world
